# 🧟 ZOMBIES DEFENSE.IO

**Survive endless hordes of zombies in this top‑down auto‑shooter!**  
Level up, choose powerful upgrades, and see how long you can last.

---

## 🎮 Play Now

Just open the [`index.html`](index.html) file in any modern browser – no server required.  
Or [play online here](#https://pankajtiwari-art.github.io/ZOMBIES-DEFENSE.io/) (if hosted).

---

## 🕹️ Features

- **Endless Wave Survival** – Each wave gets tougher with more zombies.
- **Auto‑Aim & Auto‑Shoot** – Focus on movement; the game handles aiming (can be toggled).
- **6+ Zombie Types** – Walkers, Runners, Tanks, Spitters, Screamers, and the ghostly **Phantom**.
- **Deep Upgrade System** – Choose from 23+ unique upgrades (common, rare, epic) with stacking levels.
- **Loot Drops** – Temporary power‑ups like Haste, 2x Damage, Shield, Freeze, and more.
- **Mobile Support** – On‑screen joystick for touch devices.
- **Customizable Settings** – Sound, auto‑aim, auto‑shoot, and difficulty slider (7 levels).
- **Minimap** – See zombies, XP orbs, and loot at a glance.
- **Particle Effects & Screen Shake** – Juicy feedback for hits, kills, and explosions.
- **Local Leaderboard** (Coming soon) – Track your best runs.

---

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Move** – WASD / Arrow keys or on‑screen joystick.
2. **Shoot** – Automatically aims at the nearest zombie (if auto‑aim is on).  
   *Manual aim:* turn off auto‑aim and use mouse/touch direction.
3. **Collect** – Blue XP orbs to level up. Yellow loot boxes grant temporary buffs.
4. **Survive** – Avoid zombies; your health regenerates slowly if you have the Regen upgrade.
5. **Level Up** – Every level presents 3 random upgrades. Choose wisely – some can be upgraded to level 5.
6. **Wave Clear** – After each wave you get a short break and a free level‑up.

## ⚙️ Settings

Click the **SETTINGS** button in the main menu to adjust:
- **Sound** – On/Off
- **Auto‑Aim** – On/Off
- **Auto‑Shoot** – On/Off
- **Difficulty** – 7 levels (EASY 1 … ULTRA HARD) – affects zombie health, speed, spawn rates, and composition.
